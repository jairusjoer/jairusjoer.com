## Outline

- **Resilience – surviving shocks in the supply chain**
  - Package manager compromises, registry‑level worms, and maintainer phishing as the new ~~normal~~.
  - Practices: minimal trusted base, local caches/mirrors, rapid dep swapping, compartmentalisation.
- **Sustainability – surviving the economics of AI and cloud trust**
  - Costs of training/inference, rising compute prices, and energy footprint making “everything through one frontier API” brittle over time.
  - Practices: smaller models, hybrid/local inference, cost visibility, avoiding hard coupling to a single vendor’s roadmap.
- **Sovereignty – owning your ability to change your mind**
  - Design for “vendor and registry reversibility”: interchangeable registries, pluggable AI backends, local‑first fallbacks, explicit provenance pipelines.
  - This is where you can bring in local‑first, self‑hosted models, and personal/organizational control planes as the practical expression of both resilience and sustainability.
